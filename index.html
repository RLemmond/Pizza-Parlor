Welcome to Ricardo's Pizza Parlor

Founded in 1920 by the visionary great-grandmother, Isabella Rossi, Ricardo's Pizza Parlor has been a family-owned business passed down through four generations. Isabella, a courageous Italian immigrant, arrived with nothing but her determination, three children, and a single suitcase. Through hard work, passion, and an unwavering commitment to quality, Ricardo's Pizza Parlor has become a beloved institution in the community.
